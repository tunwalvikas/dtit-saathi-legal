2.Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide and improve Saathi. The categories below reflect what the App actually collects.

2.1 Account information

When you sign up, we collect your email address and a password. Authentication is handled by our infrastructure provider, Supabase; your password is stored by Supabase in hashed form and is never visible to us.

2.2 Spiritual profile and onboarding information

During onboarding and in your profile, you may provide: display name, gender, age range, country, family situation, occupation, spiritual tradition or lineage (sampradaya), spiritual level, the practices you follow, your current life situation, primary concerns, emotional state, spiritual goals, and a free-text description of those goals. This information is used to personalise the guidance the App provides. Some of it reveals religious or philosophical beliefs and is treated as sensitive — see Section 12.

2.3 Conversations and content you create

We collect the messages you send to the Saathi AI guide and the App’s responses, including any feedback you give (for example, marking a reply as helpful). We also store practice data you generate: japa (mantra) sessions, sadhana (practice) logs, and scripture bookmarks.

2.4 Voice recordings

If you use voice input, the App records short audio clips on your device and sends them to our transcription provider to convert speech to text. The resulting text is processed like any other message. Audio is sent for the sole purpose of transcription.

2.5 Birth-chart (kundali) information

If you use the kundali or horoscope features, you may provide birth details for yourself and, optionally, family members: name, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth (including approximate coordinates), and contextual details such as marital status, number of children, career stage, and areas of concern. This data is used solely to compute the astrological chart and generate the related reports you request.

2.6 Location information

For the panchang (almanac) and “Temples Near You” features, the App uses your device’s location while the App is in use, with your permission, to provide locally accurate astronomical data and to find nearby temples. Location is used at the time of the request and is not used to track you.

2.7 Diagnostic and technical information

To keep the App stable, we collect crash reports and error diagnostics through our monitoring provider. These may include technical details such as device model, operating system version, and the technical context of an error.

2.8 Notification preferences

If you enable the daily shloka reminder, we store your chosen reminder time and language preference. Daily reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

3.How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you securely.
  • Provide the App’s features — the conversational guide, daily shloka, scripture library, japa counter, sadhana tracker, panchang, kundali and horoscope, and temple discovery.
  • Personalise guidance using your spiritual profile and the context of your past conversations.
  • Maintain conversation memory so the guide can refer to earlier discussions without you re-explaining your situation.
  • Improve the relevance of scripture retrieval based on the feedback you provide.
  • Monitor, diagnose, and fix technical problems and keep the App secure.
  • Communicate with you about your account, security, and important changes to the App or this policy.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Our legal basis for processing is your consent (which you may withdraw — see Section 8), the performance of our service to you, and our legitimate interest in keeping the App secure and functional, as permitted by applicable law.

4.How the AI guide works

Saathi’s guide uses artificial intelligence. When you send a message, the App retrieves passages of scripture relevant to your question and sends them, along with your message, your spiritual profile, and short summaries of your earlier conversations, to a third-party AI provider that generates the response. Responses are intended for spiritual reflection and education. Saathi does not provide medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice, and its responses are not a substitute for professional help. AI-generated content may occasionally be inaccurate; please use your own judgement.

Some of our service providers process data on servers located outside India, including in the United States. Where your personal data is transferred internationally, we take steps to ensure it remains protected in line with this policy and applicable law. By using the App, you understand that your information may be processed in countries other than the one in which you reside.

7.Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for as long as needed to provide the App. Specifically:

  • Account, profile, conversation, practice, and kundali data are retained until you delete the relevant item or your account.
  • Voice recordings are used transiently for transcription and are not retained as audio after the transcription is produced.
  • Diagnostic data is retained for a limited period for debugging and security purposes.

When you delete your account, we delete or irreversibly anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain certain information to comply with legal obligations.

8.Your rights and choices

Subject to applicable law, including India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you and obtain a summary of how it is processed.
  • Correct, complete, or update inaccurate or incomplete data — much of this you can do directly in the App’s profile screens.
  • Delete your personal data and your account (see Section 9).
  • Withdraw consent at any time, after which we will stop the related processing; this does not affect processing already carried out.
  • Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.
  • Grieve — raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see Section 16), and escalate to the Data Protection Board of India if unresolved.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@dtitglobal.com. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

9.Account and data deletion

You can delete individual conversations, practice logs, and kundali profiles directly within the App. You may also request deletion of your entire account and all associated personal data.

How to delete your account: use the in-App account-deletion option in your profile settings, or email info@dtitglobal.com from your registered email address with the subject “Delete my account”. We will action verified requests within a reasonable period.

10.Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit between the App, our server, and our providers.
  • Database-level access controls that restrict each user’s data to that user.
  • Storage of authentication tokens in your device’s hardware-backed secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • Server-side handling of all sensitive credentials; no provider secret keys are stored in the App itself.
  • Access limited to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While we work hard to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11.Children’s privacy

Saathi is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are below 18, please do not use the App or provide any personal information. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without the consent required by law, we will take steps to delete it. A parent or legal guardian who believes their child has provided us personal data may contact us at info@dtitglobal.com.

12.Sensitive personal data

To provide spiritually relevant guidance, Saathi necessarily processes information that may reveal your religious and philosophical beliefs and practices, and — through the kundali features — your birth details. We treat this information as sensitive. We process it only to provide the features you choose to use, only with your consent, and we do not use it for advertising or sell it to anyone. You may choose not to provide this information, though some features may then be unavailable.
